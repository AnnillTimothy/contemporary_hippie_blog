Our Story

Ancient Wisdom, Modern Living

We believe wellness isn't a trend — it's a birthright. Contemporary Hippie was born to bridge the gap between age-old holistic practices and the demands of a modern world.

Our Mission

Empowering Conscious Choices, One Article at a Time

We started Contemporary Hippie because we were tired of wellness content that was either too esoteric to be practical or too commercial to be trusted. We wanted a space where real health science meets heartfelt storytelling.

Every article we publish is researched with intention. Every product we recommend has been vetted by our team. We're not here to sell you a lifestyle — we're here to help you design one that actually feels good.

From adaptogens to mindfulness, from sustainable skincare to whole-food nutrition — we cover the topics that matter to people who care about what they put in, on, and around their bodies.

What We Believe

Our Core Values

Rooted in Nature

We believe the earth provides everything we need to thrive. Our recommendations always start with what's natural, sustainable, and time-tested.

Backed by Science

Intuition is powerful, but evidence matters. We bridge traditional wisdom with peer-reviewed research so you can make informed decisions.

Radically Honest

No sponsored content disguised as editorial. When we recommend a product, it's because we genuinely believe in it — and we'll always tell you why.

Sustainability First

From the brands we feature to the way we run our business, environmental responsibility is woven into everything we do.

Inclusive Wellness

Wellness is for everybody and every body. We champion accessibility and believe that feeling good shouldn't require a luxury budget.

Mindful Living

Beyond products and routines, we advocate for a slower, more intentional way of being — where presence is the ultimate wellness practice.

"The body heals with play, the mind heals with laughter, and the spirit heals with joy."
— Our Wellness Philosophy
